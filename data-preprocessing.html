Data Preprocessing

Data preprocessing is a critical step in Machine Learning, also, it's one of the first steps when building a model, so I figured it would be a great place to start.

In this section, I show you my first look at data preprocessing under the gaze of Machine Learning. I apply techniques such as normalization, outlier detection and show you different ways of handling missing values on a dataset (selecting a subset of attributes, filtering examples, replacing missing values, amongst others). All of this using RapidMiner Studio and its wide variety of useful operators.
